CLUB_INFO.DAT

Cyber Sentinels is the official cybersecurity club of Kings Engineering College (KEC), established to foster a community of passionate individuals dedicated to understanding, implementing, and advancing cybersecurity practices.

We are a diverse group of students, researchers, and enthusiasts who believe in the power of knowledge sharing and hands-on learning. Our club serves as a platform for members to explore various domains of cybersecurity including ethical hacking, network security, cryptography, digital forensics, and secure software development.

Through workshops, CTF competitions, guest lectures, and collaborative projects, we aim to prepare our members for the challenges of the ever-evolving digital landscape.

VISION

To become the leading cybersecurity community in academia, producing skilled professionals who can safeguard the digital infrastructure of tomorrow.

  • Pioneer cybersecurity education in the region
  • Bridge the gap between academia and industry
  • Create ethical hackers and security researchers
  • Build a safer digital world through knowledge

MISSION

To provide practical, hands-on cybersecurity training and create opportunities for students to develop real-world security skills.

  • Conduct regular workshops & training sessions
  • Participate in national & international CTFs
  • Collaborate with industry professionals
  • Promote responsible disclosure practices
